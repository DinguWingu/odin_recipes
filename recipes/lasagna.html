Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is one of the most popular lasagna Resepies for lasagna in the Allrecipies community. This is due to the customisability of this resrecipie. John Chandler submitted the Recipe to the website more than 20 years ago.


Like stated before, this lasagna recipe is incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need:

  • Meat: sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness to the sause.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

step-by-step guide

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

The lasagna should be covered with goil for 25 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Then another 25 minutes without the foil. Finallly let it rest in room temperature for 15 munutes before cutting into it.
